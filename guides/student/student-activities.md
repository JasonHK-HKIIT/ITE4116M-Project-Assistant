# Student Activities

The Student Activities feature allows you to discover and explore campus activities by asking the AI assistant. The assistant searches the MyPortal activities database and returns matching results based on your query.

## Searching for Activities

In the chat, ask a question such as:

- *"Are there any sports activities this month?"*
- *"What badminton activities are available?"*
- *"Show me activities in Tsing Yi campus."*
- *"Find activities related to music."*

The assistant will search for matching activities and list them in the chat.

## Filtering Your Search

You can refine your search by providing additional details:

| Filter | Example |
| ------ | ------- |
| **Keyword** | *"badminton"*, *"coding"*, *"drama"* |
| **Activity type** | *"sports"*, *"arts"*, *"community service"* |
| **Campus** | *"Tsing Yi"*, *"Tuen Mun"* |
| **Discipline** | *"music"*, *"engineering"* |

For example:

- *"Find sports activities at Tsing Yi campus."*
- *"Show me community service activities related to the environment."*

## Understanding the Results

Each activity listing shows:

| Field | Description |
| ----- | ----------- |
| **Title** | The name of the activity |
| **Type** | The category of the activity |
| **Date** | The start and end date of the activity |
| **Campus** | The campus where the activity takes place |
| **Venue** | The specific venue or room |
| **Discipline** | The related academic or interest discipline |
| **Vacancy** | Whether places are still available or the activity is full |
| **Description** | A summary of the activity |

### Example Response

> **Basketball Tournament 2024**
> Type: Sports
> Date: 2024-10-15 to 2024-10-16
> Campus: Tsing Yi
> Venue: Sports Hall
> Discipline: Physical Education
> Vacancy: Available
>
> Join our annual basketball tournament open to all students. Form a team of five and register before 10 October.

## Joining an Activity

The assistant provides information about activities, but registration is managed through MyPortal directly. To join an activity:

1. Note the activity name from the assistant's response.
2. Log in to MyPortal.
3. Navigate to the **Student Activities** section and search for the activity.
4. Complete the registration form.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How many activities are shown at once?**
By default, the assistant returns up to 5 matching activities. You can ask for more by saying *"Show me more activities"* or *"Give me 10 results"*.

**Why can I not see all activities?**
The results shown come from the MyPortal activities database and reflect activities that are currently active or upcoming. Past activities are not shown.

**The activity I want is shown as Full. What can I do?**
Check MyPortal regularly for cancellations that may free up a place, or contact the organiser of the activity directly.
