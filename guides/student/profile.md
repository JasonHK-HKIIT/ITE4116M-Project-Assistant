# My Profile

The Profile feature lets you view your personal account and student details as stored in MyPortal. You can retrieve this information at any time by asking the AI assistant.

## Viewing Your Profile

In the chat, ask a question such as:

- *"Show me my profile."*
- *"What is my username?"*
- *"What programme am I enrolled in?"*
- *"What is my current class?"*

The assistant will fetch your profile from MyPortal and display the details in the chat.

## Profile Information

Your profile includes two sections:

### Account Information

| Field | Description |
| ----- | ----------- |
| **Name** | Your full name (family name and given name) |
| **Username** | Your MyPortal login username |
| **Role** | Your account role (e.g. student) |
| **Chinese name** | Your Chinese name, if provided |

### Student Details

| Field | Description |
| ----- | ----------- |
| **Institute** | The institute you belong to (e.g. HKIIT) |
| **Campus** | Your assigned campus |
| **Gender** | Your gender as recorded in MyPortal |
| **Date of birth** | Your date of birth |
| **Nationality** | Your nationality |
| **Mother tongue** | Your mother tongue language |
| **Mobile number** | Your registered mobile phone number |
| **Telephone number** | Your registered telephone number |
| **Address** | Your residential address |
| **Current class** | Your current class code and academic year |
| **Programmes** | The academic programme(s) you are enrolled in |

## Updating Your Profile

Your profile information is managed by MyPortal. If any details are incorrect, please contact the student registration office or update them directly in MyPortal.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change my profile picture?**
Profile pictures are managed in MyPortal. Please log in to MyPortal and go to your account settings to update your avatar.

**Why is some information missing from my profile?**
Some fields are optional or may not have been entered during registration. Contact the student registration office if you believe required information is missing.

**Is my personal data secure?**
Yes. Your profile is retrieved directly from MyPortal using your authenticated session. The data is displayed only in your personal chat and is not shared with other users.
